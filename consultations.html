Hall Decor and Service Consultations

Have you imagined your dream wedding your entire life? Well bring your idea to life!

Are you worried about planning an important corporate event? We'll take the stress out of planning for you!

From start to finish, Party Time is here to handle all of your special occasions. We offer FREE evet decor planning and consultations. We are happy to sit down with you and design a unique plan to fit your budget. No party is too large or small. We can help you transform any venue through complete table design, full event decorations, set-up, breakdown, and delivery!

Some of our event services include helping you to select your colors and coordinating linens. Laying out linens and overlays, placing dinnerware, glassware, silverware, and placing and folding napkins. We also rent and set up chair covers, sashes, and chair backs. We can design beautiful centerpieces and place them on each table. We can even crate unique favrs for your occasion and place them at each place setting along with votives for that finishing touch!

We decorate ceremony sites for all cultures and backgrounds including churches, altars, chuppahs, mandaps, and more! We decorate tents, aisles, podiums, stages, and off-site cereonies. We also decorate and create accessories for many cultural customs and traditions including lassos, arras, and brooms. One of our favorite things to do is to decorate a very specific main table for the guests of honor and sweetheart tables for the bride and groom!

We also create fresh or silk flower arrangements for any occasion. We do centerpieces, bouquets, boutonnieres, corsages, arches, altar arrangements, altar baskets, pew decor, flowers for the aisles, pillars and columns with flowers, special sweetheart tables, floral arrangements, graduation leis, and more!

We truly are your AFFORDABLE one-stop shop for all your special event needs!
